ReadMe file supporting the data file "Field_2020_2021", supporting the manuscript "Bio-residuals of industrial insect production as novel sustainable crop fertilizer", authored by Barragan et al.
________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Contact: d.mertens.sci@gmail.com
________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

The excel file contains two data sheets, for the two years during which data was collected (2020 and 2021 respectively) 
________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Plant_Code: 
Factor with 359 levels denoting a unique plant code as identifier.
	
ID:	
Factor variable with 20 levels denoting the replicate per block-by-treatment combination.

Treatment: 
Factor with six levels denoting soil treatment. Treatments include soil amended with exuviae of one of three insect species, namely house cricket (HC), black soldier fly (BSF) or mealworm (MW), plants grown in soil amended with shrimp chitin (CHT) or organic fertilizer (OF), or plants grown in unamended soil as control (C) 
	
Block:
Factor with three levels denoting the block in which plants were planted. Blocks were each planted two weeks apart. 
	
WidthT1: 
Continuous decimal variable representing the width of the plant at its widest point (leaf tip to leaf tip) 5 weeks after sowing. 
	
WidthT2:
Continuous decimal variable representing the width of the plant at its widest point (leaf tip to leaf tip) 7 weeks after sowing. 
	
NflowT1:
Continuous integer variable representing the number of flowers plants produced 7-9 days after the first flower opened. 
	
NflowT2:
Continuous integer variable representing the number of flowers plants produced 14-16 days after the first flower opened. 
	
HeightT1:
Continuous integer variable representing the height of plants (ground to the tip of the tallest inflorescence) 7-9 days after the first flower opened. 

HeightT2:
Continuous integer variable representing the height of plants (ground to the tip of the tallest inflorescence) 14-16 days after the first flower opened. 
	
Sow_To_Flower:
Continuous decimal variable representing the number of days since the seed was planted until the first flower opened
	
Nseeds:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of seeds produced by the plant
	
Tsyrphid:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of syrphid flies recorded 7-9 days after the first flower opened.

TOFlies:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of other flies recorded 7-9 days after the first flower opened.
	
THBee:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of honey bees recorded 7-9 days after the first flower opened.
	
TSBee:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of solitary bees recorded 7-9 days after the first flower opened.
	
TBBee:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of bumblebees recorded 7-9 days after the first flower opened.
	
Nflowvisited1:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of flowers visited by pollinators 7-9 days after the first flower opened.
	
Tsyrphid2:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of syrphid flies recorded 14-16 days after the first flower opened.
	
TOFlies2:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of other flies recorded 14-16 days after the first flower opened.
	
THBee2:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of honey bees recorded 14-16 days after the first flower opened.
	
TSBee2:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of solitary bees recorded 14-16 days after the first flower opened.
	
TBBee2:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of bumblebees recorded 14-16 days after the first flower opened.
	
Nflowvisited2:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of flowers visited by pollinators 14-16 days after the first flower opened.
	
TotalPollinators:
Continuous integer variable representing the total number of pollinators recorded, independent of the group.
	
FPP:
Continuous decimal variable representing the average number of flowers visited per tracked pollinator.
